## Graph Patterns You Must Know for Google

Before solving questions, master these core patterns:

### Breadth First Search (BFS)
- Used for **shortest path in unweighted graphs**
- Common in grids, levels, minimum steps problems  

👉 [Practice BFS](https://hackmnc.com/topic/breadth-first-search/leetcode-interview-questions)  


---

### Depth First Search (DFS)
- Used for **traversal, backtracking, cycle detection**
- Important for trees + graphs  

👉 [Practice DFS](https://hackmnc.com/topic/depth-first-search/leetcode-interview-questions)  


---

### Topological Sort
- Used in **Directed Acyclic Graphs (DAG)**
- Common in course schedule & dependency problems  

---

### Union Find (Disjoint Set)
- Used for **connectivity problems**
- Detect cycles in undirected graphs  

---

### Dijkstra's Algorithm
- Used for **shortest path in weighted graphs**
- Frequently asked in system-like problems
